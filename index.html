General
If checked, TimeDock will reduce animations (saves some battery life).
You must have animations on for this to work.
If checked, TimeDock will automatically open when you place your Pebble on your TimeDock (or plug your Pebble into any charger). Warning: this feature uses the background worker.
Weather
If checked, Upside Down will use weather instead of fahrenheit.
If this field is NOT blank, TimeDock will attempt to use the inputted location for grabbing weather. Otherwise, GPS will be used.
